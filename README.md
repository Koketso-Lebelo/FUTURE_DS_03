# E-Commerce Funnel & Conversion Analysis Dashboard | Power BI


An interactive **Power BI dashboard** analyzing the full customer purchase journey in an e-commerce platform:  
**View → Add to Cart → Purchase**

This project identifies major drop-off points, measures conversion efficiency, highlights top-performing categories and brands, and provides actionable recommendations to optimize the sales funnel and increase revenue.

##  Project Objectives

- Map and analyze the e-commerce conversion funnel
- Identify where users drop off the most
- Measure stage-wise and overall conversion performance
- Determine which categories and brands drive the highest revenue
- Provide data-driven strategies to improve conversion rates

## 📊 Dataset

**Dataset Used:** eCommerce behavior data from a multi-category online store  
**Link:** [eCommerce Events History in Cosmetics Shop](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mkechinov/ecommerce-behavior-data-from-multi-category-store)

This dataset contains user events including **view**, **cart**, and **purchase** actions, along with product categories, brands, and pricing information.

##  Tools & Technologies

- **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development and interactive visuals
- **DAX** – Custom funnel metrics, conversion rates, and calculations
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and modeling

##  Key Metrics

- **Total View Sessions**: 235,000
- **Cart Sessions**: 9,000
- **Purchase Sessions**: 15,000
- **Overall Conversion Rate**: 6.24%
- **Cart Abandonment Rate**: 21.66%
- **Total Revenue**: 310M+

##  Dashboard Highlights

- **Interactive Funnel Visualization** (View → Cart → Purchase)
- Stage-wise drop-off analysis and conversion rates
- Revenue and conversion rate by product category
- Top-performing brands by revenue and conversions
- KPI summary cards with key performance indicators
- Filters for deeper segmentation

##  Key Insights

- The **largest drop-off** occurs at the **View → Cart** stage
- Once users add items to cart, they have a high likelihood of completing the purchase
- Overall conversion rate remains relatively low at **~6.24%**
- Revenue is heavily concentrated in the **Electronics** category
- A small number of brands contribute the majority of total revenue

##  Business Recommendations

- Optimize product pages and improve user engagement to boost **Add to Cart** rates
- Implement retargeting campaigns for users who viewed products but didn’t add to cart
- Focus marketing budget and promotions on high-performing categories and brands
- Continue refining the checkout experience (already performing well)
- Test personalized recommendations and urgency tactics on product detail pages

##  Key Takeaway

The primary bottleneck in the e-commerce funnel is **early-stage engagement** (View → Cart). While the checkout process is efficient, improving product discovery and cart addition can significantly lift overall conversions and revenue.
